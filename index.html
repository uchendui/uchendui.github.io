Ikechukwu "Ike" Uchendu

I am a doctoral student at Harvard University in the Edge Computing Lab. Prior to this, I worked as an AI Resident at Google Brain.

Previously, I received a BS/MS in computer science from Michigan State University.

My main research interest is in figuring out how reinforcement learning agents can acquire new skills as fast as possible.
